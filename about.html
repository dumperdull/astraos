About AstraOS

AstraOS is a command-line-first operating system designed for developers and power users. It focuses on speed, minimalism, and customizability, giving you full control over your environment.

Why AstraOS?

  • Optimized for performance on low-end hardware.
  • Fully open-source and community-driven.
  • Designed for automation, scripting, and productivity.
